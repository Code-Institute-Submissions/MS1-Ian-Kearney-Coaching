"All golfers have different backgrounds, abilities, movements, individual goals but most importantly are on different pathways. I hope I can adapt and manage my role to best suit their individual golfing needs at a specific moment in time. For me that's the beauty and sometimes difficulty in coaching"

Ian Kearney

Education & Training

Ian is a PGA qualified golf coach and has been awarded the prestigious PGA Irish Trainee of the Year on two occasions while completing his training at Doonbeg Golf Club in Co. Clare. Since turning professional, Ian has spent time working internationally, the highlight of which was during his time working in Germany for Taylormade Adidas Golf at their global headquarters. During this time, he was privileged to work with many Taylormade Staff Touring Professionals including Martin Kaymer during his journey to becoming the world’s number 1 Golfer in 2011.

Coaching Career

On returning to Ireland Ian has spent several years developing his craft as a golf teaching professional while also completing his Masters degree in Sports Coaching at the renowned Loughborough University specialising in instruction and coaching philosophy. He is the current Golfing Union of Ireland Under 16’s National Coach and the Lead Coach for Connacht Golf, positions he has held since 2016.
