Pricing

Single Lesson

A one to one session with Ian for 30 minutes where he will analyze issues and explain what is going wrong and a few 'quick fixes' to help your game short term.

This is a good opportunity to see whether you like both golf lessons and Ian's style of coaching. You can then decide if you would like to continue lessons.

€40

Short-game Masterclass

A one to one session with Ian for an hour where you will go through all things short-game. He will analyze all areas within the first few minutes and decide how long to spend on each aspect from there.

e.g 10 minutes analzing, 20 minutes chipping, 10 minutes bunkers, 20 minutes putting.

€60

"Tune-Up Three"

Three 30 minute sessions with Ian at your preference of frequency (fortnightly recommended) where he will:

  1. Identify Weaknesses and what you would like to fix in your game.
  2. Implement changes and explain mechanics of your swing.
  3. Fine tune swing and create tailored warm-up routine.

€100

Winter Program

Work with Ian over the space of 4 months receiving a lesson every 2 weeks. In this time Ian will acess your goals and create a tailored winter program just for you.

Each lesson will be 30 minutes and will go through previous lessons exercises reinforcing them and also providing new exercises. You will have an accuracy test in your first lesson, fourth lesson and final lesson to show your progress over the months.

€250
