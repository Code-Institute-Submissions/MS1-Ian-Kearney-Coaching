During this time, he also provided support to Taylormade Staff professionals including the world number one at the time, Martin Kaymer.

Ian has worked in Trump International Golf Links Doonbeg since 2011 and completed his Masters degree in Sports Coaching at the renowned Loughborough University specialising in instruction and coaching philosophy.
